The Geothermal Contracts layer indicates the location and spatial extent of Geothermal Service Contracts/Geothermal Renewable Energy Service Contracts that are at the development stage. This phase of the contract involves “the development, production, or utilization of renewable energy (RE) resources, including the construction and installation of relevant facilities up to the operation phase of the RE facilities” (DOE, DC2007-07-0011 Chapter II Sec. 2). RE contracts have an initial term of 25 years that is renewable for not more than 25 years.
