The Petroleum Contracts layer shows the location and acreage holding of contractors seeking to develop or are currently engaged in petroleum production across the Philippines. Under a Petroleum Service Contract (SC), the contractor performs the exploration work and, in the latter phase, the operation of the resource field on behalf of the Government. The exploration period under a service contract is seven (7) years extendable to three (3) years, while initial production operations are allowed within a period of 25 years.
